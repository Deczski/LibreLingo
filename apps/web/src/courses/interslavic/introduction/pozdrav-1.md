# Lekcija 1 | Lesson 1

## Osnova | The basics:

*   Ja jesm = I am
*   Ty jesi = You(singular) are
*   On jest = He is
*   Ona jest = She is
*   Ono jest = It is
*   My jesmo = We are
*   Vy jeste = You(plural) are
*   Oni sut = They(masculine) are
*   One sut = They(feminine) are
