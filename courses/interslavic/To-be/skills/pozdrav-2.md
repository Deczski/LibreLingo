# Lekcija 2

## Osnova | The basics:

- Ja jesm byl = I was
- Ty jesi byl = You(singular) was
- On byl = He was
- Ona byla = She was
- Ono bylo = It was
- My jesmo byli = We were
- Vy jeste byli = You(plural) were
- Oni byli = They(masculine) were
- One byli = They(feminine) were
